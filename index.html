Our games are suitable for all ages, including children. We do not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details from any user, including children.

The third-party SDKs listed below may collect a device advertising ID for attribution and analytics. Parents or guardians can disable this at any time via: Android Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID. Deleting the advertising ID stops interest-based data collection.

Our games contain no in-app purchases, no social features, and no user-generated content.
